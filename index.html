About Schlong Society!

The Schlong Society is a collection of 10,000 unique randomly generated NFTs, particularly made to help men facing Prostate and Testicular Cancer. Our schlongs possess their own unique traits and attributes. The foundation on which Schlong Society has been built upon is to help men suffering from both devastating forms of cancer. Our donation based system is meant to assist these men fight the tough fight.

Partnered Charities

Find answer to your questions!

What percentage of sales are going to the charitable organizations?

20% of our total sales will be donated to charitable organizations who work for the welfare of men.

How can I become a part of the Schlong Society?

To become a part of our society or to join our mission, head over to our social media groups and start contributing there! You can join us at Discord, Twitter, and Instagram

How can I join the pre-sale?

To join the pre-sale of our NFTs, you need to first complete the activities listed on our “get-whitelisted channel” located on our discord

What organizations will the donations go to?

NFT backgrounds marked with the light blue prostate cancer ribbon in the upper left corner indicate donations will go to ZERO -- The End of Prostate Cancer foundation. - NFT backgrounds marked with the purple testicular cancer ribbon the upper left corner indicate donations will go to the Testicular Cancer Society

Any plans for metaverse location building?

Plans for a Metaverse community hub will take place in The Sandbox. We believe this will be the easiest MMO P2E game to be a part of for the majority of the community.
